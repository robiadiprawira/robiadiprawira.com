Halal Local
Project Information

Project Date:2019

Client:PT Astrajingga Inovasi Digital

Link:https://halallocal.com/

Description
Halal Local is an application that helps Halal travelers find Halal restaurants around the world. It not only facilitates the search for Halal restaurants but also offers Halal travelers the opportunity to provide reviews about the Halal restaurants they visit. Additionally, users can check the Halal certification held by the restaurant. Halal Local has previously received numerous awards from both domestic and international sources.
My role and the achievements, which include:

  • Contributing to research on UI Design and features of the Halal Local application.

  • Participating in designing the user interface.

  • Representing Halal Local at events to promote the activities of Muslim organizations involved in organizing the Hajj.

  • Assisting Halal Local in winning the Hi-Start award in Australia.

  • Serving as a support team for the Halal Local team while in Australia.
